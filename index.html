ABOUT US

Burgeria foodtruck

Burgeria is a local food truck that have been established since 2019 by six young local entrepreneur. It only started just as a simple dream by six people back in their students life. The idea to start a food truck is because of the loves of food among the community. The main focus of the food truck is to sell a western food that connected with a local touch. It is because food truck can be considered as a trendy things for people nowadays. The creation of Burgeria is because with a rise in youth spending, middle-class expenditure, conscious consumerism, mobile lifestyle and requirement of food on the go, the food truck model has become quite a rage among the local people. Besides that, Burgeria can be considered as a quite unique food truck that sell western type of food because there are quite unique type of food that can be found from the menu.


Burgeria started their food truck journey on Klang Valley. It can be found on certain area such as Shah Alam or Pantai Dalam on weekdays. But on weekend the food truck can be found either on Subang Jaya or Sri Hartamas based on the week of the month. It will be set for the first two weekdays of the month at Shah Alam and the last two weekdays it will be at Pantai Dalam. It will be applied as the same for the weekend as the first two weekend of the month at Subang Jaya and the last two weekends at Sri Hartamas. The change of location is because to ensure that the popularity of Burgeria can keep on increasing and become much more famous in the future and can open up their own restaurant.
